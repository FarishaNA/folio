Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. What We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Your chosen username
  • Your password (stored as a BCrypt hash — never in plain text)
  • Notes and folders you create within the app

We do not collect email addresses, IP addresses, or any tracking data.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Folio service — authenticating your account and storing your notes. We do not analyze, sell, or share your data with any third party.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway. Data is not encrypted at rest beyond password hashing, so avoid storing sensitive personal information in notes.

4. Cookies and Sessions

Folio uses a single session cookie to keep you logged in. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

5. Your Rights

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from your profile page. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.

6. Third-Party Services

Folio is hosted on Railway (Railway Privacy Policy). No other third-party services are used.

7. Changes

If this policy changes materially, the "Last updated" date above will reflect it.

8. Contact

Questions? Open an issue on the GitHub repository.
